Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026. We value your privacy and process personal data in line with India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP).

What we collect

We collect the data you provide when you register and use the portal:

  • Account: name, email address, password (stored securely), and role (Employer or Jobseeker).
  • Employers: company, designation, and job listings you post.
  • Jobseekers: skills, location, and job applications you submit.

Why we use it

Your data is used to run the job portal: to create and manage your account, show and match jobs, let employers view applications, and improve the service.

How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You can request account deletion by contacting us.

Your rights

You can access and update your profile and settings from the dashboard. You may request correction or deletion of your personal data in line with applicable law, including the DPDP Act.

Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact the administrator of this portal.
